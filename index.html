Industry Q&A

Which mining regions do you serve?

Our operations are centered in the Mpumalanga coal belt (Eendracht/Embalenhle), but we provide industrial and mining support across Gauteng and nationwide for large-scale contracts.

How verified is your CSD registration?

Madlangempisi Holdings is a fully verified, active supplier on the Central Supplier Database (Supplier Number: MAAA1564432). We meet all CIPC, SARS, and Treasury compliance markers.

What are your core mining support capabilities?

We specialize in Mining and Quarrying support, specialized material handling, plant maintenance, and high-integrity industrial supply chains including machinery procurement.

Is your company Level 1 B-BBEE compliant?

Yes. We are 100% black youth-owned and operate as an Exempted Micro Enterprise (EME) with a Level 1 B-BBEE contribution status, aiding our clients' procurement scorecards.

How do you handle health and safety?

Safety is our apex priority. We maintain strict adherence to the Mine Health and Safety Act (MHSA) and the OHS Act, ensuring all personnel are certified and all sites are risk-managed.

Executive Overview

MADLANGEMPISI HOLDINGS (PTY) LTD (Registration: 2025/073170/07) is a premier South African industrial entity, strategically headquartered in Mpumalanga. Established with a vision to revolutionize the resource sector's supply chain, we provide specialized support services that merge technical rigor with logistical precision.

As a 100% black youth-owned firm, we represent the vanguard of South African engineering leadership. Our methodology is built on the "Zero-Harm" principle, ensuring that all mining and civil operations meet international health and safety benchmarks (MHSA & OHS Act).
